Fanny He

PhD Student at the Department of Computer Science of the University of Bath, in the Mathematical foundations of computation group.
My supervisors are Willem Heijltjes and Guy McCusker.

Research interests

I am currently working in lambda-calculi and their denotational models, and in proof theory, especially from a Curry-Howard perspective. My research interests include:

  • Lambda-calculi
  • Proof theory
  • Denotational semantics
  • Logic
  • Infinitary term rewriting
